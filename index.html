About Me

Growing up, I was always interested in art, design, and technology. My senior year of high school I took an AP Computer Science course, where I was first introduced to coding. I decided to study Web Development at Lipscomb University in Nashville, Tennessee. Web Development allows me to integrate my passion for design and interest in computing.

Learning about accessibility and usability led me to research user experience and user interface. Reading stories from designers and users emphasized the importance of designing and building applications around their users, not around the company’s ideas of how applications should be made.
